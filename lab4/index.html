Lab 4- Psuedocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

For this lab we had to practice psuedocoding with both everyday tasks and simple computer games. We also had to practice commenting in js and using both html and css.

Problems

It was for the most part pretty straightforward, I think the biggest problem I experienced was doubting if the language I was using for the psuedocode was correct. I think as I gain more familiarity with psuedocoding, the conventions will become more obvious.

Task 1:

Lighting a candle:

  • Find a candle and a matchbook
  • Light the match
    • Take the match out
    • Press the match head on the striker
    • Quickly swipe the match head against the striker
  • Light the candle
    • Hold the head of the match against the candle wick
    • Once the wick is lit, pull the match away
  • Blow out the match
